The trust layer

An SDR agent's worst failure is not missing a signal. It is confidently pitching a wrong one. Three hard gates make that structurally impossible.

GuaranteeMechanismFailure it prevents
Right businessEntity resolution: the lead's website must match its name and location before anything else runsPitching based on a same-named stranger's news
Real, fresh factsEvery claim's source is fetched and corroborated, with a 12-month recency check. Citations come from Google Search grounding metadata, never from generated textCongratulating someone on stale or hallucinated news
Never repeatsA change ledger plus per-lead cooldowns: rescans only surface what is newSpamming the same signal twice
